## Customer Journey Map: Health-Data-Guard

| Phase   | Trigger Event | Friction Points | User Emotions | Opportunities to Delight | Metric |
|--------|---------------|------------------|----------------|----------------------------|--------|
| **Aware** | New state-level data privacy law announced (e.g., CA B.O.B., TX HB 4512); audit failure; vendor compliance request; breach scare | - Uncertainty about applicability of new regulations<br>- Overwhelm from legal jargon<br>- Lack of internal expertise in cross-state compliance | Anxious, overwhelmed, cautious | - Deliver hyperlocal compliance radar (email/alerts) with plain-English summaries<br>- Publish "Compliance Heatmap" showing risk exposure by state/sector | **Awareness Rate**: % of target orgs (health/ed) that recognize Health-Data-Guard as a solution within 30 days of regulation release |
| **Consider** | Leadership mandates compliance roadmap; RFP issued; comparison of vendors begins | - Difficulty mapping tools to specific regulatory clauses (e.g., "Does this cover NY SHIELD Act encryption requirements?")<br>- Fear of vendor lock-in or over-engineering<br>- Budget constraints for non-core infrastructure | Skeptical, analytical, risk-averse | - Offer instant regulatory gap analysis (upload policy → get control matrix)<br>- Publish third-party validation reports (SOC 2, state-specific attestations)<br>- Show side-by-side comparison vs. HIPAA-only tools | **Consideration Lift**: % of evaluated prospects who shortlist Health-Data-Guard after using self-serve compliance scanner |
| **Try** | Onboarding pilot team (e.g., school district IT, clinic admin); sandbox access granted | - Steep learning curve for policy configuration<br>- Integration delays with legacy EHR/IS systems<br>- False positives in data classification | Frustrated, time-pressured, hopeful | - Pre-load state-specific templates (e.g., "CT Student Privacy Rules v1.2")<br>- 1-click integration with Clever, Epic, Canvas<br>- AI-powered anomaly triage: auto-dismiss 80% of false alerts | **Time-to-First-Value**: <4 hours from signup to first compliant workflow deployed |
| **Adopt** | Full deployment across departments; audit preparation begins | - Change management resistance from staff<br>- Reporting gaps for auditors<br>- Scaling issues across multi-campus/multi-clinic environments | Relieved, vigilant, proud | - Auto-generate auditor-ready compliance packets (PDF + API)<br>- Role-based training micro-modules (5-min videos per job function)<br>- Live compliance score dashboard with executive summary | **Adoption Depth**: ≥80% of data workflows under policy enforcement within 60 days |
| **Expand** | Passed audit; new state operations launched; merger/acquisition | - Manual replication of policies across states<br>- Need for student/parent consent management<br>- Demand for AI red-teaming of policies | Confident, proactive, strategic | - One-click policy portability across jurisdictions<br>- Auto-synthesize consent forms from regulation text<br>- Offer "Red-Team as a Service" using Thai-language attack simulation (leverage existing Axentx product) | **Expansion Ratio**: 2.3x ARR within 12 months via cross-state & cross-product adoption |
