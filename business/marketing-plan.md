# Marketing Plan

## Positioning
**Primary:** "Compliance-as-a-Service for healthcare and education: AI-driven framework ensuring state-level regulatory adherence for sensitive data."
**Alternatives:**
1. "Secure your sensitive data with AI-powered compliance automation for healthcare and education sectors."
2. "AI-driven compliance framework tailored to state-level regulations for healthcare and education data security."
3. "Ensure regulatory adherence with our AI-powered data security framework for healthcare and education."

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profiles)
1. **Sarah Thompson, Compliance Officer**
   - **Daily Job:** Ensures the healthcare organization adheres to state and federal regulations, manages compliance documentation, and conducts regular audits.
   - **Budget:** $50,000 - $100,000 annually for compliance tools and services.

2. **Michael Chen, IT Director**
   - **Daily Job:** Oversees IT infrastructure, ensures data security, and implements compliance measures for educational institutions.
   - **Budget:** $30,000 - $70,000 annually for IT security and compliance solutions.

3. **Emily Rodriguez, Data Privacy Manager**
   - **Daily Job:** Manages data privacy policies, conducts risk assessments, and ensures compliance with state-level regulations for sensitive data handling.
   - **Budget:** $40,000 - $80,000 annually for data privacy and compliance tools.

## Channels
1. **LinkedIn Ads**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $150 - $250 per acquisition
   - **Rationale:** Targets compliance officers, IT directors, and data privacy managers with tailored ads and sponsored content.

2. **Webinars and Online Events**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $100 - $200 per acquisition
   - **Rationale:** Educates potential customers on the importance of compliance and demonstrates the product's capabilities through live demos and Q&A sessions.

3. **Industry Conferences and Trade Shows**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $200 - $350 per acquisition
   - **Rationale:** Provides face-to-face interactions with key decision-makers in the healthcare and education sectors, building trust and showcasing the product.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1-4:** Educational content on state-level regulations and the importance of compliance in healthcare and education.
- **Week 5-8:** Case studies and success stories from early adopters.
- **Week 9-12:** Product demos, webinars, and detailed feature explanations.
- **Week 13-16:** Customer testimonials, ROI analyses, and comparative studies with competitors.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize product features and pricing, create marketing collateral, and set up marketing channels.
- **D-0:** Official product launch, kick off marketing campaigns, and host a launch webinar.
- **D+30:** Gather initial customer feedback, optimize marketing strategies, and refine product based on feedback.
- **D+90:** Achieve target DAU and MRR, expand marketing efforts to new channels, and scale customer acquisition.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** 500 by D+90
- **$MRR Target:** $50,000 by D+90
