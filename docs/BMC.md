# Business Model Canvas – **Health Data Guard**

| **Building Block** | **Details** |
|--------------------|-------------|
| **Value Proposition** | • **Zero‑code policy enforcement** for health‑care workloads in Kubernetes clusters.<br>• Real‑time compliance with HIPAA, GDPR, and local regulations via a lightweight runtime integration layer.<br>• Centralized policy store with immutable audit logs, reducing audit‑prep time by ≥ 50 %.<br>• Seamless Helm‑based deployment – no code changes required for existing micro‑services.<br>• Built on proven open‑source components (e.g., OPA, Kyverno) with enterprise‑grade support and SLA guarantees. |
| **Customer Segments** | 1. **Healthcare providers & hospitals** operating containerized workloads (EMR, imaging, tele‑health).<br>2. **Life‑science SaaS vendors** handling PHI/PII in the cloud.<br>3. **Regulated enterprises** (pharma, biotech) needing Kubernetes compliance tooling.<br>4. **Managed service providers (MSPs)** offering compliant Kubernetes platforms to health‑tech clients. |
| **Channels** | • **Direct sales** – enterprise account executives & solution architects.<br>• **Partner ecosystem** – cloud ISVs, MSPs, and Kubernetes platform vendors (e.g., Rancher, OpenShift) integrate Health Data Guard as a certified add‑on.<br>• **Marketplace** – Helm chart in public Helm Hub and private registries (AWS Marketplace, Azure Marketplace).<br>• **Developer community** – GitHub, webinars, and open‑source contributions to drive adoption and feedback. |
| **Revenue Streams** | 1. **Subscription SaaS** – tiered annual plans (Starter, Professional, Enterprise) covering runtime license, policy‑store SaaS, and support.<br>2. **Consumption‑based add‑ons** – per‑cluster or per‑policy‑evaluation pricing for high‑throughput environments.<br>3. **Professional services** – implementation, custom policy authoring, and compliance audit assistance (hourly or fixed‑price).<br>4. **Support & SLA contracts** – premium 24/7 support, guaranteed response times, and dedicated success managers. |
| **Cost Structure** | • **R&D** – core engine, policy language extensions, security hardening, CI/CD pipelines.<br>• **Cloud infrastructure** – policy‑store SaaS (object storage, DB), monitoring, and logging.<br>• **DevOps & Release Ops** – Docker image builds, Helm chart publishing, CI runners.<br>• **Sales & Marketing** – channel partner programs, webinars, compliance certifications.<br>• **Support & Success** – tiered support staff, ticketing system, knowledge base.<br>• **Legal & Compliance** – audits, certifications (HIPAA, ISO 27001, SOC 2). |
| **Key Resources** | • **Core runtime engine** – Go/Rust binary integrated as a Kubernetes Admission Controller & sidecar.<br>• **Policy Store** – highly‑available, tamper‑evident storage (e.g., etcd + S3 with WORM).<br>• **Compliance knowledge base** – curated policy templates for HIPAA, GDPR, HITRUST.<br>• **Engineering talent** – security engineers, Kubernetes experts, SREs.<br>• **Intellectual property** – patents on policy‑evaluation optimization and immutable audit trail. |
| **Key Activities** | • **Product development** – maintain runtime, Helm chart, CI pipelines, and policy language extensions.<br>• **Compliance updates** – continuously map new regulations into policy templates.<br>• **Partner integration** – certify and co‑market with cloud platforms and MSPs.<br>• **Customer success** – onboarding, training, and audit‑readiness workshops.<br>• **Security operations** – vulnerability scanning, penetration testing, and incident response. |
| **Key Partners** | • **Open‑source policy engines** – Open Policy Agent (OPA), Kyverno (for rule execution).<br>• **Cloud providers** – AWS, Azure, GCP (for managed Kubernetes services & marketplace distribution).<br>• **Compliance auditors** – third‑party firms that validate our HIPAA/GDPR compliance claims.<br>• **Security tooling vendors** – SIEM/EDR partners for integrated alerting.<br>• **Industry associations** – HIMSS, HL7 for standards alignment and market credibility. |
| **Unfair Advantage** | • First‑mover offering **runtime‑only** enforcement with **zero‑code** integration for health‑care workloads.<br>• Proprietary **immutable audit log** that is cryptographically signed, enabling instant audit readiness.<br>• Deep **regulatory expertise** embedded in policy templates, reducing time‑to‑compliance for customers. |
